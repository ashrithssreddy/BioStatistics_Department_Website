
<h6><b>$2 Million NIH Grant Awarded to Dr. Emily Zhang</b></h6><br>
<p>We are proud to announce that Dr. Emily Zhang has secured a $2 million grant from the National Institutes of Health (NIH) for her research on health outcomes in underrepresented communities. This grant will support groundbreaking studies in biostatistics and social determinants of health over the next five years.</p>
